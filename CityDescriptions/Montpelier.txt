Montpelier is a city in the U.S. state of Vermont that serves as the state capital and the shire town of Washington County, Vermont, in the United States. As the capital of Vermont, Montpelier is the site of the Vermont State House, seat of the legislative branch of the Vermont government. The population was 7,855 at the 2010 census. The city is the smallest state capital by population in the US. The Vermont College of Fine Arts and New England Culinary Institute are located in the city.
